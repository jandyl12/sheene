Our Mission

BAGOnista Adventures is your gateway to the breathtaking beaches and islands of Maribago Lapu-Lapu City. Our mission is to promote local tourism and share the joy of Ma-yo Beach, Bluegarden Resort, and Mundo Island.

We believe in creating unforgettable memories by showcasing the natural beauty and unique experiences our locality has to offer. Join us in celebrating the paradise in Maribago Lapu-Lapu City.
