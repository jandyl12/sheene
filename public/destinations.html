Ma-yo Beach

The Serene Escape

Ma-yo Beach is the epitome of tranquility. Known for its gentle waves and soft, sandy shores, it's the perfect place to unwind, read a book, and soak up the tropical sun. The local community is warm and welcoming, offering a glimpse into the peaceful island life.

Unwind on Golden Shores

Feel the soft sand beneath your toes and listen to the gentle lull of the waves. Ma-yo Beach offers an idyllic setting for pure relaxation. Find a quiet spot under a swaying palm tree, or simply enjoy a leisurely stroll along the shoreline.

Crystal Clear Waters & Marine Life

The serene waters of Ma-yo Beach are not just for swimming; they are also a haven for vibrant marine life. Grab your snorkeling gear and discover colorful fish darting among coral formations just a few meters from the shore.

From the city center, take a tricycle to the main port (approx. 15 minutes, ₱50). From there, a short 10-minute boat ride (₱100 per person) will take you directly to the beach.

Dry Season (Nov-May)

Best time for clear skies and the calmest seas, perfect for all activities.

Wet Season (Jun-Oct)

Fewer crowds but expect occasional rain showers. Still beautiful!

Bluegarden Resort

The Hub of Adventure

Dive into adventure with the endless blue waters of Bluegarden Resort. This location is your hub for excitement, offering a wide array of thrilling activities. It's the ultimate destination for adrenaline junkies and water lovers.

Thrill-Seeking Watersports

Bluegarden Resort is synonymous with high-octane water activities. Experience the rush of jet skiing, feel the wind in your hair while parasailing, or explore nearby islands on an exhilarating island hopping tour.

Relaxation and Refreshments

After an action-packed day, unwind at Bluegarden's vibrant beachside bar. Enjoy refreshing cocktails and delicious meals with stunning ocean views. It's the perfect place to share stories of your day's adventures.

Bluegarden is accessible via a 20-minute private boat hire from the city port. Package tours often include direct transport. Expect to pay around ₱800-₱1000 for a round-trip boat for up to 6 people.

Weekdays for Less Crowds

Visit on weekdays to avoid the largest crowds and enjoy shorter waits for activities.

Morning for Calm Waters

The water is calmest in the morning, making it ideal for parasailing and other water sports.

Mundo Island

Where Magic Happens

Witness unforgettable sunsets that paint the sky in a spectacle of color. This magical island is where lasting memories are made, offering picturesque views and a peaceful atmosphere.

Spectacular Sunsets

Mundo Island is renowned for its mesmerizing sunsets, where the sky transforms into a canvas of fiery oranges, soft pinks, and deep purples. Find a comfortable spot and watch as nature puts on a spectacular show.

Stargazing & Tranquil Nights

As darkness falls, Mundo Island reveals another kind of magic. Far from city lights, the night sky comes alive with countless stars. Enjoy an evening of peaceful stargazing for a truly serene experience.

Mundo Island is typically the final stop on island hopping tours that start from Bluegarden Resort. You can also hire a private boat for a dedicated sunset tour (approx. 25-minute ride).

Golden Hour Arrival

Arrive about an hour before sunset to see the light change and secure the best spot.

Low Tide Exploration

Visiting during low tide reveals beautiful sandbars and tide pools, perfect for exploring.
